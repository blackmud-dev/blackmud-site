BlackMUD: The Age of Rebirth!

The light of Phaet and the shadow of Nixil have both been seen in the realm again. Change has come alive in the world, and the struggle between order and chaos, good and evil are renewed.

Enter the Realm

History

Entia possesses a deep and rich unique history to explore, merging elements of nation-state, racial, and religious strife.

Races

Countless races wander the lands of Entia, with many unique species that can only be found in the world of Entia.

Classes

BlackMUD has 17 classes and sub-classes for players to experience. Each with its own abilities, strengths, and weaknesses.

Pantheon

Merging mono and polytheistic elements, the Pantheon is entertwined in the struggle for or against the Unmaking.

Policies

Like any community, BlackMUD has a set of norms of behavior, based on OOC decency and respect, that are required of players.

Maps

The world of Entia is a dark medieval fantasy setting filled with a variety of unique and exciting places to explore.
