Lore and History

Entia possesses a deep and rich unique history to explore, merging elements of nation-state, racial, and religious strife.

World

The world of Entia is a dark medieval fantasy setting filled with a variety of unique and exciting places to explore.

Races

Countless races wander the lands of Entia, with many unique species that can only be found in the world of Entia.

Classes

BlackMUD has 17 classes and sub-classes to experience. Each with its own abilities, strengths, and weaknesses.

Pantheon

Merging mono and polytheistic elements, the Pantheon is entertwined in the struggle for or against the Unmaking.

Policies

Like any community, BlackMUD has a set of behavioral norms, based on decency and respect, that are required.
